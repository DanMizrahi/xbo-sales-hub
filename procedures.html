New Lead Onboarding Procedure IMPORTANT

Corporate Registration Link

https://www.xbo.com/corporate/register

For Sales Team — New Clients

  1. Collect details: Company Name + Email they'll register with
  2. Share OB link: Send the corporate registration link
  3. Notify Marcel on Teams: Post in "New Onboarding Leads" group with Company Name + Email
  4. Lead assignment: Marcel verifies and assigns in CRM

For Support/Sales — Existing Client via Group Chat

Same steps as above, but also include which company group the new lead belongs to.

For Referrals Team

Same steps, but clarify which referral the new lead came from.

Important Notes

  • No notification = auto-rotation: Lead goes to standard rotation if not claimed
  • Check CRM regularly: Filter "Assigned to" with your name
  • Your responsibility: Once assigned, the lead is yours
  • Test accounts: Use "TEST" in company name

Core Deal Information
  • Owner
    Auto-assigned by CRM
  • Title
    Format: Company Name or Company – PAY
  • Company Group
    Select parent organization from list
Volume & Services
  • Expected Volume
    Use K/M format (e.g., 250K, 1.2M)
  • Services
    Multi-select: CEX, PayX, OTC, Custody, Other
  • SPOT Fee
    Enter as number (e.g., 0.25 for 0.25%)
  • PayX Fee
    Enter as number (e.g., 1.5 for 1.5%)
Lead Source Information
  • Referred By
    Name or "Self-sourced"
  • Source Channel
    LinkedIn, Conference, Referral, Inbound, etc.
  • Source Channel ID
    Specific identifier (profile URL, @handle, event name)
  • Source Origin
    Country or region
Status & Contact
  • Contact Person
    Link actual Pipedrive Person record (not text)
  • Money Flow
    Document INBOUND and OUTBOUND fund flows

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Volume as "250000" → Use 250K format
  • SPOT Fee as "0.25%" → Enter 0.25 (number only)
  • Source Channel ID blank → Always include specific identifier
  • Money Flow as "Standard" → Document actual flow
  • Contact Person as text → Link actual Pipedrive record
