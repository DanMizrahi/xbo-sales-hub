Fireblocks

Enterprise custody & infrastructure
Their Strengths
  • Enterprise-grade custody (MPC technology)
  • Strong brand recognition
  • Extensive DeFi integrations
  • Large customer base (1800+ institutions)
Their Weaknesses
  • NO exchange ‚Äî custody only
  • Complex, expensive pricing
  • Long implementation (6-12 months)
  • No payment processing
  • No credit line offering
üéØ How to Win

Position XBO as the all-in-one alternative. Fireblocks requires multiple vendors (exchange, payments, banking). We provide everything. Emphasize faster time-to-market (4-6 weeks vs 6-12 months) and simpler pricing.

BCB

BCB Group

Crypto-focused banking
Their Strengths
  • UK FCA authorized
  • Strong fiat banking rails (BLINC)
  • Good OTC desk
  • Institutional focus
Their Weaknesses
  • UK-centric (Brexit complications for EU)
  • No MiCA license yet
  • Limited white-label options
  • No credit line
  • No earn/staking products
üéØ How to Win

For EU clients: MiCA compliance is the key differentiator. BCB's UK focus creates friction post-Brexit. Also highlight our Credit Line for CFD brokers and CaaS white-label for EMIs wanting to launch fast.

M

Mercuryo

Crypto payment gateway
Their Strengths
  • Easy widget integration
  • Good fiat on-ramp
  • Cards available
  • Competitive for small volumes
Their Weaknesses
  • No OTC desk
  • No institutional trading
  • Limited liquidity for large volumes
  • No credit line
  • Basic white-label only
üéØ How to Win

Mercuryo is retail-focused. For B2B clients needing institutional features (OTC, Credit Line, deep liquidity), XBO is the clear choice. Emphasize our $5M per transaction capability and dedicated dealing desk.

B

BitPay

Legacy crypto payments
Their Strengths
  • Long track record (2011)
  • Good brand recognition
  • Simple merchant integration
  • BitPay card popular with consumers
Their Weaknesses
  • US-centric, limited EU support
  • No MiCA, no EU license
  • No OTC or institutional services
  • No banking (IBAN)
  • Aging technology, slow innovation
  • No white-label options
üéØ How to Win

BitPay is legacy technology with US focus. For any EU client or anyone needing institutional features, XBO wins easily. Highlight MiCA compliance, full product suite, and modern API. They're not really competing in the same segment.
